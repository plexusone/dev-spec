# Market Requirements Document Evaluation

## Criterion: market_understanding (30%)

**Market Understanding** - Market is well-researched

### GO
Deep market understanding:
- Market size quantified
- Trends identified
- Competitive analysis thorough
- Market dynamics understood

### WARN
Basic understanding:
- Some data present
- Gaps in analysis

### NO-GO
Poor understanding:
- No market data
- Assumptions not validated

---

## Criterion: opportunity_clarity (30%)

**Opportunity Clarity** - Business opportunity is clear

### GO
Clear opportunity:
- Problem well-defined
- Solution fit evident
- Timing justified
- Differentiation clear

### WARN
Partial clarity:
- Opportunity stated but vague
- Differentiation unclear

### NO-GO
Unclear opportunity:
- No clear value proposition
- Why now not answered

---

## Criterion: audience_definition (25%)

**Audience Definition** - Target audience is defined

### GO
Well-defined audience:
- Segments identified
- Personas developed
- Needs documented
- Size estimated

### WARN
Basic definition:
- Audience mentioned
- Lacks detail

### NO-GO
Undefined audience:
- No clear target
- Generic "everyone"

---

## Criterion: business_case (15%)

**Business Case** - Financial viability shown

### GO
Strong business case:
- Revenue model clear
- Costs estimated
- ROI projected
- Risks identified

### WARN
Basic case:
- Some financials
- Incomplete picture

### NO-GO
No business case:
- No financial analysis
- Viability unknown
