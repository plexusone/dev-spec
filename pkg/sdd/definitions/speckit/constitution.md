# Constitution Document Evaluation

## Criterion: clarity (40%)

**Clarity** - Principles are clear and understandable

### GO
Constitution is crystal clear:
- Principles are concise and memorable
- No ambiguity in guidance
- Easy to apply to decisions
- Consistent terminology

### WARN
Mostly clear:
- Some principles vague
- Minor ambiguities

### NO-GO
Unclear constitution:
- Principles are confusing
- Contradictory guidance
- Cannot apply to decisions

---

## Criterion: actionability (35%)

**Actionability** - Principles guide decisions

### GO
Highly actionable:
- Principles inform design choices
- Clear do's and don'ts
- Can resolve disagreements
- Applicable to daily decisions

### WARN
Partially actionable:
- Some principles too abstract
- Hard to apply in practice

### NO-GO
Not actionable:
- Purely aspirational
- Cannot guide decisions
- Too vague to apply

---

## Criterion: completeness (25%)

**Completeness** - Covers key areas

### GO
Comprehensive coverage:
- Technical principles defined
- Quality standards set
- Constraints documented
- Trade-off guidance included

### WARN
Partial coverage:
- Missing some areas
- Key constraints unstated

### NO-GO
Incomplete:
- Major gaps
- Critical areas missing
